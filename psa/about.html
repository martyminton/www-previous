About

Integrity First... Quality Always.

ProSol Associates, L.L.C. (PSA) is a dynamic and growing small business providing high-quality professional services to the US government. Our mission is to create value for customers before, during and after service delivery while maintaining the highest moral code. We conduct high-touch engagements in which senior management remains involved from proposal through acceptance and beyond to build and maintain long-term relationships. As a result, we have quickly built a reputation for service excellence, integrity and speed.

PSA holds Top Secret facility clearance and employs numerous TS/SCI -cleared employees. PSA consists of personnel from the armed forces in addition to other federal organization and commercial entities. Our staff is well rounded to support any mission found within our skill set lane. We recruit, develop and retain the most talented and customer-focused employees in the industry and continually invest in their training and professional development.

PSA provides focused expertise in Intelligence Operations and Analysis, Information Technology, Contact Centers Solutions and Program and Project Management to organizations such as the Defense Intelligence Agency (DIA), Transportation Security Administration (TSA), Department of Labor (DOL) and US Marine Special Forces, Special Operation Command (MARSOC).

Part of the ProSol family of companies, PSA also is dedicated to supporting the community through organizations like PS Charities which raises and distributes funds to charities that support military families, fight cancer, and engage in other worthy causes.
