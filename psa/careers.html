Careers

PSA has developed a diverse team of talented professionals. We offer a highly competitive compensation package and the kind of interesting work that gives employees a real opportunity to make a difference for their country and fellow citizens.

We also foster an environment of professional and personal growth to enable employees to best meet customer needs. This includes training and professional development (such as strategic intelligence, technology and project management certification), as well as the pursuit of advanced degrees.

PSA professionals are flexible, customer-focused, self-starters who work hard but also enjoy life. Work-life balance is very important at PSA and we take time to celebrate our mutual successes at social and community events.
