10/3/09 – Jazz it Up! PSA supports PS Charities with Silent Auction
PS Charities had their first annual silent auction supporting the GWU Breast Care center. The George Washington University Medical Faculty Associates Breast Care Center offers a comprehensive program for early detection, diagnosis, and treatment of breast cancer, as well as services for those at high risk for breast cancer. Combining state-of-the-art technology with superior medical and surgical care, the Center is staffed by a team of nationally recognized specialists trained to evaluate benign and malignant disease. Patients have access to the range of breast imaging modalities and the newest minimally invasive techniques used for diagnosis; an entire evaluation can be completed during a single office visit. In addition, the Breast Care Center offers an array of support services for patients and families.

This auction was held at Ruth’s Chris Steak House in Arlington, VA. PSA is very proud to be a sponsor for this event. It was a huge success raiding $20,000 for a very worthy cause. Contracts PS Charities!


9/27/09 – PSA awarded Prime contract with US Marine Special Forces, Special Operation Command
In early September PSA was awarded their first prime contract in support of US Marine Special Forces, Special Operation Command (MARSOC). We were asked to support the MARSOC RAVEN Deployment Certification Exercises (DCE). These exercises are designed to test MARSOC skills in operating environments that are similar to those experienced on deployment. This was a crital milestone for PSA as it was our first prime contract supporting a customer who has a unique mission that aligns very well with the skill sets of our company.

7/24/2009 – PSA support Professional Solutions in 5th Annual Charity golf tournament
Professional Solutions (ProSol) hosted thier 5th annual charity golf tournament Friday, July 24, 2009 at Fort Belvoir Golf Club. The beneficiary of our event is the Tragedy Assistance Program for Survivors, in memory of Major Doug Zembiec—a fallen warrior and a man of principle who was killed protecting our nation on May 10, 2007. TAPS is the 24/7 tragedy assistance resource for ANYONE who has suffered the loss of a military loved one, regardless of the relationship to the deceased or the circumstance of the death.

In typical fashion ProSol doubled their charitable donation from the previous year and raised $50,000. As a sponsor, PSA was proud to participate in the tournament and will continue to support it for many years to come.


5/18/2009 – PSA awarded contract supporting DIA
PSA was awarded a 3 year contact to support DIA’s Strategic Counterintelligence Directorate (SCID) mission as a subcontractor to Lockheed Martin. Our Analysts will identify, neutralize and exploit foreign intelligence and security services, terrorists, and insurgents targeting U.S. and Coalition interests. This contract continues PSA’s support to the Intelligence Community.


3/4/2009 – PSA begins support to the TSA Secure Flight Mission
PSA is awarded our first contract with the Transportation Security Administration supporting a new program called Secure Flight. Secure Flight is a behind the scenes program that enhances the security of domestic and international commercial air travel through the use of improved watch list matching. By collecting additional passenger data, it will improve the travel experience for all airline passengers, including those who have been misidentified in the past. PSA is proud to be a part of this contract.
